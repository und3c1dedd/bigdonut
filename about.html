ABOUT US

Welcome to BIG-D where every bite is a celebration flavor and quality! At BIG-D, we're passionate about crafting delectable treats that bring joy to every moment. From our handcrafted donuts to our refreshing bevrages, each item on our menu is carefully curated to ensure a delightful experience for our customers. Located in the bustling heart of San Isidro, Nueva Eicja we've been delighting locals and visitors alike with our delectable handcrafted dounuts since 2024!

OUR STORY

BIG-D was born from dream of our founder, John Nelson Parungao, whose passion for baking and a desire to spread joy through food sparked the creation of our shop. Using cherished family recipes enhanced with a touch of modern flair, BIG-D opened its doors to share the love and joy of great tasting donuts with everyone.
